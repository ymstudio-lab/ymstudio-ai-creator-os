## Public Boundary

Allowed here:

- App screenshots and demo videos.
- Public demo notes that describe what the asset shows.
- Reproducible scripts for generating public demo frames.

Not allowed here:

- Platform descriptions, comment drafts, tags, community posts, or channel-specific copy.
- Commercial pages, long-form product drafts, pricing, packaging, or revenue strategy.
- Internal Codex, Claude, Hermes, Discord routing, or personal workflow instructions.
- API keys, OAuth details, tokens, account IDs, customer data, or private contracts.
